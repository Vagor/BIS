CDGDC Verification

CDGDC, standing for China Academic Degrees and Graduate Education Development Center, is an administrative department directly under the Ministry of Education, operating under the joint leadership of the Ministry of Education (MoE) and the Academic Degrees Committee of the State Council (ADCSC), and possessing the independent qualification of legal entity. CDGDC started to verify the Chinese degree certificate and other educational background since 2000. The higher education verification CDGDC conducts includes: all levels and kinds of degree certificates, transcripts, studying certificates and other background documentations issued by institution including higher education institutions and scientific research institutions that belong to national education system within mainland of China (exclude Hong Kong and Macau).

Currently, CDGDC is the only authority to verify the domestic higher education certificates and transcripts. It is also recognized by mainstream American education verification institutions, colleges and universities, especially for WES and ECE, who require student to do the CDGDC Verification first and then issue the final verification report after receiving the CDGDC Verification result.

CDGDC Verification Template is as follow:

CDGDC Verification Procedures:
NOTE: CDGDC Verification Report is required for obtaining WES Report. If you apply for WES Report, please pay extra attention to Step Five.

Step1

Log in www.cdgdc.edu.cn, and click on “China Qualification Verification”.


 

Click on “Apply for Verification”.


 

Step 2

Click on the Register button and fill in your email address to start the real-name registration.

As for uploading the scanned copy of your ID, please write the following statement: This scanned copy is for CDGDC Verification only, and the exact date as shown in the picture below.


After submitting your ID copy, you will receive an automatic email including the activating link to which you can activate and complete the registration.

 

Step 3

Log in the system after your registration. Click on “Apply for Verification” to start an application.


Click on “I have read and agree to all the above agreement” to the next step.

Select “Overseas Study” in the “Purpose” box.



Step 4
Fill the detailed information sequently as “Higher Education- Undergraduate- International Summer Session Transcript”, and upload the electronic version of your transcript (Note: The upload file must be in picture format). 


Step 5

Fill in the detailed emailing address. If it requires mailing the report to WES, please select “WES” in the “Report Receiving Institute”.


Note: You should fill in the form above after obtaining the WES reference number. The CDGDC report without reference number will be neglected by WES. 

Complete the payment after confirming your emailing address.



The end of procedures.
