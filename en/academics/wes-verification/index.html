WES Verification

World Education Services (WES) is the largest credential evaluation institute in America, providing evaluations for those who intend to apply for jobs or admission to an educational institution in America or Canada. With a history of more than 40 years, WES’s evaluation reports are extensively accepted by academic institutions in these two countries. In 2009, China Academic Degrees and Graduate Education Development Center (CDGDC) established a partnership with WES. Since then, holders of diplomas and academic records in China (excluding Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan) shall firstly apply for evaluation reports of CDGDC before going to WES.
Here are the WES application guidance:

Application Procedures:
NOTE: CDGDC Verification Report is required for obtaining WES credential evaluation. In order to make the application procedure smoothly, student should submit the application for WES credential evaluation and obtain the Reference Number first, and then apply for CDGDC Verification. Make sure to fill the reference number in Step Five of CDGDC Verification Procedure, or else the CDGDC report will not be processed by WES.

Step 1. Log into the official WES website

Log in http://www.wes.org, and click on “Apply for a WES Credential Evaluation”. Select “China” in the “Required Documents” and then choose the Country of your Destination.


Step 2.Create an Account
 


Fill in your basic information to create an account. The following detailed information is as below:

 

1.Personal Info

Enter a valid address to ensure delivery, normally your home institution.

 

2.Your Education

Select “General Academic High School” in Institution Type and type in the school you graduated from.
 

3.Your Evaluation

Selected “to continue my education” for the question “What is the primary purpose for this evaluation”, and then tick the box of “course-by-course evaluation” under service WES ICAP.

Tips: “course-by-course evaluation” under service WES ICAP is recommended as the report will be stored and sent to the institution.

4. Service & Fees

Confirm all the service you required and fees included.

 

5. Payment Info


Step 3. Check the Result

After completing the online application and payment, you can check the processing status at https://www.wes.org/appstatus/ in three days. If additional research, correspondence or verification (i.e. CDGDC Verification) is required, it will be shown in the status. Processing time to receive a CDGDC Verification is normally 20 days for express service, and a WES credential evaluation is 7 business days after receipt, review and acceptance of all documents and payment in full. Therefore, the whole process of obtaining WES credential evaluation lasts for about one and a half month or be shortened to 20 days. The application fee is approximately $300.
