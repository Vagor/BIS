We offer scholarships for outstanding students in our programs:

Students who rank the top 1% will be offered First Class Scholarship and awarded $1000 for each.
Students who rank between 1% and 2% will be offered Second Class Scholarship and awarded $500 for each.
 
The scholarships will be based on comprehensive evaluations on grade points, attendance, professor’s recommendation, and etc.

Paul Magelli Scholarship

SIE establishes  a scholarship named Paul Magelli, to commemorate the professor of UIUC  Paul Magelli and thanks for his contribution to SIE.
 
1. Each Student who signs up for course Macroeconomics or Microeconomics will be awarded $50 for each. 
 
2. Each student from UIUC who gets two or more grade A in SIE summer program, will be eligible to share in the $2000 awards pool at the end of the summer in 2017.
