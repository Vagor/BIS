Refund Procedure:

1.Please submit the refund application in the online system.

2.Refund for course price difference or accommodation, please contact us via wechat: sie_consultant.

Refund for On-campus Housing:

1.Students enrolled in ECNU-4 program who would like to cancel the order for On-campus Housing, should submit the application for accommodation refund before May 13, 2018; application after 00:00 on May 14, 2018 will not be accepted.

2.Students enrolled in BJTU-4/ECNU-5 program who would like to cancel the order for On-campus Housing, should submit the application for accommodation refund before Jun 15, 2018; application after 00:00 on Jun 16, 2018 will not be accepted.


Note:

1.We will refund through online system not by cash; admission fee occurred during online payment/bank transfer will be deducted from the refund.

2.BJTU/ECNU ISS reserve the right to cancel the courses which do not meet the enrollment of 5 people, and students who have enrolled in these courses will be informed by email.

3.All the refund applications will be processed within two weeks.

4.Deadlines above are 24:00 Beijing Time except for specific notice.

5.For more inquiries, please contact us by 400-666-5576.
