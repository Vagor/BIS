Positions

Program Assistant
If you are good at communicating in intercultural settings and would love to attend to minute details in everything, then you are the one!
 
Duties:
1.       Assist Program Director with daily administration of this summer program.
2.       Plan and organize events such as local trips, social nights for students and business program.
3.       Prepare orientation for students, and report concerns and suggestions from faculty and students to the SIE Administrative staff to improve the program.
4.       Assist professors and their families to solve problems encountered in daily life and accompany them to go on a trip upon request.
5.       One of the Program Assistants will be worked as a Resident Assistant as well, who is in charge of all the accommodation related work.
What we offer:
1.       We will offer you a good amount of salary for your devoted work.
2.       You are most likely to get reference from professor for your outstanding service.
3.       To learn more than program operation from our experienced management team.
4.       RA will be provided accommodation for free.
 
Teaching Assistant
Requirement:
Undergraduate sophomore or above or graduate students of related majors.
 
Duties:
1.       Assist professor in grading of homework, papers and exams, and attend relevant courses.
2.       Hold 1 office hour session per course each day, and help professor with other course-related work including printing lecture notes, photocopying notes, etc.
3.       One of the Teaching Assistants will be worked as Writing Instructor as well, who shall provide office hour from Monday to Friday, assisting students to revise the papers and meet the writing requirements from professors.
What we offer:
1.       We will offer you a good amount of salary for your devoted work.
2.       You are most likely to get reference from professor for your outstanding service.
3.       WI will be provided accommodation for free.

Campus Representative
Duties:
1.       Get connected with students and promote SIE summer program.
2.       Extend marketing channels in coordination with online marketing
3.       Organize Info Session to further promote the program
4.       Collect credit-transfer policy and information and instruct our students in credit transferring.
5.       Extend and maintain the long-term cooperation with student’s union and institutions in home university.
What we offer:
1.       We will offer you basic salary plus bonus for your devoted work.
2.       You are most likely to get reference from Marketing Director for your outstanding service.
3.       To get a comprehensive improvement in communication, management, and etc.
